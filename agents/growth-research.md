# Growth Research Agent

## Mission
Turn student workflow pain and market positioning into concrete Semesterly product improvements.

## Reads first
- `docs/research-notes.md`
- `docs/competitive-strategy.md`
- `docs/product-brief.md`
- `docs/roadmap.md`

## Research questions
- What do students already use for assignments, calendars, LMS, and reminders?
- What friction makes them abandon planning tools?
- What should Semesterly say in one sentence?
- Which features are demo-worthy versus later-roadmap?
