# Competitive Strategy

## What strong apps do well

### MyStudyLife

- Student-specific calendar.
- Classes, tasks, exams, study sessions in one place.
- Smart reminders.
- Cross-device sync.
- Pomodoro/focus tools.
- AI timetable scan.
- Grade tracking.
- School/family features.

### Todoist

- Fast task capture.
- Today and Upcoming views.
- Natural-language task entry.
- Filters.
- Calendar planning.
- Strong trust positioning.

### Notion

- One workspace.
- Flexible pages/databases.
- Search.
- AI assistant layer.
- Strong templates/community.

## Semesterly edge

Semesterly should not be another calendar.

It should be the student decision layer:

- What matters today?
- What should I do first?
- What is at risk?
- How much work do I really have?
- What did I forget?
- Can I trust the app with my data?

## Dream feature set

### Core

- Daily command center.

- Course dashboard.

### Next

- Natural-language quick add.

### Trust

- Explainable priorities.

- Server-side auth.
